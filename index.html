Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at CityU-Oxford Joint CIMDA, City University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. Hong Yan (Fellow of IEEE and IAPR). I received my Ph.D. degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2023, supervised by Prof. Yulin Huang. I was a visiting Ph.D. student at NExT Research Center, National University of Singapore, working with Prof. Tat-Seng Chua.

My research centers on multimodal perception and representation learning, with a particular focus on tackling challenges such as data scarcity, cross-modal inconsistency, and complex environments. I serve as a reviewer for top-tier journals (IEEE TIP, IEEE TNNLS, ISPRS JPRS, IEEE TGRS, etc.) and conferences (CVPR, AAAI, ICCV, ACM MM).
